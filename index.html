Our Story

About Leo Club SLTC

The Leo Club of Sri Lanka Technology Campus is a prestigious youth organization dedicated to developing leadership skills and serving our community. As part of Lions Clubs International, we're connected to a global movement of young people making a difference.

Our members come from diverse academic backgrounds, united by a common goal: to serve, learn, and lead. We believe in the power of youth to create positive change in society.
