Our Story

About Leo Club SLTC

The Leo Club of Sri Lanka Technology Campus is a prestigious youth organization dedicated to developing leadership skills and serving our community. As part of Lions Clubs International, we're connected to a global movement of young people making a difference.

Our members come from diverse academic backgrounds across the campus, united by a common goal: to serve, learn, and lead. We believe in the power of youth to create positive change in society.

"Rosa Mal Fundrasing Project

Rosamal Fundraising is a community-driven initiative dedicated to improving facilities at Maharagama Apeksha Hospital. The project aims to raise funds to support better medical equipment, patient care services, and overall hospital development.

30 Volunteers 200+ Beneficiaries
Ongoing

Green Step

Green Step is a sustainability-driven project designed to minimize plastic pollution through smart control and awareness strategies. The system promotes environmentally friendly practices by encouraging reduced plastic consumption and sustainable lifestyle choices.

16 Volunteers 500 Trees
Completed

Vidu Man Petha
